One day I realized that a bit too often I feel angry or said, especially in the office. I'm not sure how severe the problem is and decided to track it. I build this service and started using it daily to record any good or bad emotions I had. After two or three weeks I checked the statistics and see that indeed more that 50% of recorded emotions were angry or sad! Since then I try to improve my stats and things look a bit more green today...

This simple service allows you to record your emotions and check how it changed.

It can be used individually, as a personal emotions blog, or in a team to record team happiness index.

Record your mood

Record you current emotions with 1 simple click.
I learned that it is very useful to record a brief explanation with my mood.

Check statistics

It is very interesting to check, how ofter do you smile or make an angry face. Statistics can show you such information for given period of 7,14,30 or 30 days.

Show the history

Recorded information can be also presented in calendar view for 7, 14, 30 or 60 days.

Make it public

With public link feature you can easily share your current mood to your friends or your boss :-) A person using this link can see your recent emotions.
